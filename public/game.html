The main goal of this game is to get the four pawns of your color in their end zones. Upon throwing six, you can move a pawn from home to start and throw again. You may now move your pawn the amount of eyes you threw. If you move to a square which already has a pawn on it, that pawn has to move to home again. Controls: Click the button "Roll the die" when it's your turn to roll the die. Click on the pawn you want to move. If the move is invalid, the pawn won't move.
